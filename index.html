Sayf Ali

Sayf Ali (MBA, CSM, CSPO) is a Multi-Cloud, Agile Project Manager and Scrum Master/Product Owner across leading platforms such as AWS, Azure, GCP, IBM Cloud, OCI, and Salesforce. He has a 10-year proven track record of large-scale, IT implementations for Fortune 500 firms, delivering solutions for cloud adoption and migrations, data lakes/warehouses, Machine Learning, and app modernization. Sayf is meeting technical demand in industry as an Associate Full Stack Developer with a quickly growing proficiency in JavaScript and MERN, learning at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) Professional Coding Program. He has supported clients in technology, finance, healthcare, pharmaceuticals, industrial gas and chemicals, manufacturing, hospitality, and government.
